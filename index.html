// About

Who Is Michael?

👨‍💻
Biography

Michael Combrinck began his career in the tech industry, building real-world experience before following his true calling into education. With a passion for people and a drive to see students genuinely succeed, he joined Belgium Campus as a full-time lecturer.


His teaching philosophy is simple but powerful: understanding beats memorization every single time. He pushes students to think critically, build real projects, and develop the habits that actually get them hired.

💡
Teaching Philosophy

Michael believes that rote learning produces fragile knowledge. His mission is to build genuine conceptual understanding — because a developer who truly gets why something works will always outperform one who only memorised how.
